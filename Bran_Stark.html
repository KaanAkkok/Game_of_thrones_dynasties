Bran Stark

King Bran I the Broken, born Brandon Stark and commonly known simply as "Bran," is the fourth child and second son of Eddard and Catelyn Stark. Bran is a warg and a greenseer serving as the new Three-Eyed Raven. Eventually, he was crowned as the first elected ruler of the Six Kingdoms, styled as Bran the Broken, the First of His Name, King of the Andals and the First Men, Lord of the Six Kingdoms, and Protector of the Realm.


Biography

Bran is the fourth child and second son of Lady Catelyn and Lord Ned Stark of Winterfell. Bran was born and raised at Winterfell. He has an older brother Robb, a younger brother Rickon, two older sisters Sansa and Arya, and an older "bastard half-brother", Jon Snow, who is actually Bran's first cousin by his aunt Lyanna Stark. Bran was named for Ned's elder brother, Brandon, who was brutally executed by the Mad King along with Bran's paternal grandfather Rickard Stark. He is only called "Brandon" by his mother when he has done something wrong. Bran dreams of being a knight of the Kingsguard, and his favorite hobby is climbing the walls of Winterfell, using its old rooftops and passageways to get around.
